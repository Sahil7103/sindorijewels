Since 1960

Three generations, one workbench.

The name over the door has stayed the same for over fifty years — and so has the promise behind it. This is the family, and the craft, that made it.

I
1960 — The founder

The first workbench

Shri Govindbhai Ishwarbhai Soni

It began with one goldsmith, a set of hand tools and a small counter in Ahmedabad. Every order was drawn on paper, weighed on a beam balance, and delivered by hand. The rule was simple: honest gold, honest weight, no exceptions.

1990s — The second generation

Growing the craft

Shri & Shri Sushilbhai Govindbhai Soni

The next generation took the same bench and widened it — bringing in diamonds and certification, learning new techniques, and building the relationships with families who still return to us for every wedding in the house.

II
III
Today — The third generation

The atelier on CG Road

Shri Mihirbhai Sushilbhai Soni

The third generation runs the boutique at Supermall, CG Road — pairing the old craft with modern design, custom work and the reach of Instagram, while keeping the founder's rule exactly where it started.

What has never changed

The founder's rule, kept.

Fifty years and three generations later, the way we work is still the way we started: gold you can trust, work done by our own hands, and a relationship that outlasts a single purchase.
